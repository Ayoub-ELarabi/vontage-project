7 Reasons Your Link-in-Bio Is Not Converting (And How to Fix It)

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

You have the followers. You have the clicks. But nobody is buying. Here is the diagnostic checklist to fix your conversion leaks.

1. Too Many Choices

If you have 20 links, you have 0 links. Decision fatigue sets in immediately. Cap your links at 5-7 max. Archive old content.

2. Vague Link Titles

"Blog" is boring. "How to make $1000 today" is compelling. Descriptive titles earn the click.

3. Slow Load Times

If your bio link page takes 5 seconds to load (common with heavy graphical tools), 30% of users have already bounced. Vontage's static HTML loads instantly.

4. Broken Links

It sounds obvious, but when was the last time you vetted your links? A 404 error is a trust killer.

5. No Hierarchy

All links look the same. Use bolding, emojis, or positioning to highlight the ONE most important action you want the user to take.
