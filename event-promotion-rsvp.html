Event Promotion 2.0: Using Bio Links for RSVP and Tickets

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Events live and die by registration numbers. Whether it's a Zoom webinar or a warehouse rave, friction kills conversions.

The Countdown

Update your bio link title to include a countdown or date. "Live in 2 Days: Register Now". Urgency drives action.

Central Intelligence

Link to: 1. Ticket purchase (Eventbrite/Luma), 2. Venue Map (Google Maps), 3. FAQS. Keeping these all in your Vontage profile prevents users from getting lost in email threads.

Post-Event Utility

After the event, change the link immediately to "Event Photos" or "Watch Replay". The bio link lifecycle continues even after the doors close.
