Real Estate Marketing: The Realtor's Guide to Bio Links

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Realtors live in their cars and on their phones. Your clients are the same. A PDF brochure doesn't work on mobile.

Feature Listing

Change your top link weekly to "Featured Listing: 123 Maple Dr". Include a video tour link. It keeps your profile looking active and successful.

Lead Gen

"What is my home worth?" linking to a valuation calculator is the classic Realtor lead magnet. Make sure it's visible in your Vontage profile above the fold.

Scheduling

Integrate your Calendly or booking tool. "Book a Viewing" helps capture intent immediately when a buyer falls in love with a property on your Instagram.
