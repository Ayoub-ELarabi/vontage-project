How to Grow a Discord Community Using Your TikTok Bio

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

TikTok is for broadcasting; Discord is for bonding. Moving casual viewers into a private community is the holy grail of retention.

The "Exclusive" Angle

Don't just say "Join my Discord". Say "Join the VIP Chat" or "Get help with your loadout". Frame the Discord as a place of utility and exclusivity.

Using a Permanent Invite Link

Discord invite links expire by default. Make sure to generate a permanent link, or your bio link will be dead in 7 days. Add it to Vontage with the Discord purple color for brand recognition.

Gatekeeping for Quality

If you want a high-quality community, you can gate the Discord link behind a paywall (via Patreon) or a simple quiz. This filters out trolls and ensures only dedicated fans join.
