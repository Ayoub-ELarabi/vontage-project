Top 10 Bio Link Design Trends Taking Over 2026

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Design moves fast. What looked modern in 2023 looks dated today. Here are the trends dominating the bio link space in 2026.

1. Glassmorphism 2.0

Translucent backgrounds with blur effects (like Vontage uses) remain king. They feel native to iOS and Android interfaces and offer a premium, futuristic look.

2. Neo-Brutalism

Hard shadows, stark outlines, and high-contrast colors. Popular with Gen Z creators and developers. It signals "I create, I don't conform".

3. Video Backgrounds

Static images are out. Low-bitrate looping video backgrounds bring a page to life. Motion captures attention immediately.

4. Bento Grids

Inspired by Apple's promotional videos. Instead of a list of links, creators are using square and rectangular grids to organize content visually. It turns a list into a dashboard.

5. Minimalist Monochrome

Black on white. Helvetica font. No distractions. The "luxury fashion house" aesthetic is making a comeback for high-end consultants and photographers.
