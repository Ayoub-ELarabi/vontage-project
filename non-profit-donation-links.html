Non-Profits: Optimizing Donation Links for Maximum Impact

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

When urgency strikes (a disaster relief fund, for example), you need a link up in seconds. Websites crash; Vontage doesn't.

Specific Campaigns

Don't link to a generic "Donate" page. Link to "Help Hurricane Victims". Specificity increases emotional buy-in.

Transparency Reports

Link to your annual impact report. Donors want to know where the money goes. Making this info accessible builds long-term trust.

Volunteer Signups

Money isn't the only resource. Use a secondary link for "Volunteer with Us" to capture time and talent from your community.
