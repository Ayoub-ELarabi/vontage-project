5 Fatal Mistakes You Are Making With Your Bio Link

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Is your bio link chasing away visitors? Check if you are guilty of these crimes.

1. The "Link Graveyard"

Linking to a blog post from 2022 that nobody reads anymore. Prune your links. If it hasn't been clicked in 3 months, delete it.

2. Low Contrast Text

Using grey text on a black background looks cool on your fancy monitor, but is unreadable on a phone in sunlight. Accessibility comes first. Vontage uses high-contrast text by default for this reason.

3. No Profile Photo

People connect with faces. A generic logo or missing photo reduces trust. Use a high-quality headshot.

4. Linking to "Home" Instead of "Product"

If you talk about a specific shirt in your post, link to that shirt. Don't make the user hunt for it on your homepage. They won't.

5. Ignoring Load Speed

Using a tool that loads 5MB of scripts just to show 3 links. Keep it lightweight. Speed is a feature.
