SEO Backlinks: Leveraging Bio Links for Domain Authority

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Google counts links from social media profiles (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn) as signals of legitimacy. While many are "nofollow", they still drive "referral traffic", which is a ranking factor.

The "Hub" Strategy

Use Vontage as your "Hub". Link to your main website, your blog, and your key landing pages. This creates a spiderweb of fast-loading links pointing to your money pages.

Anchor Text

Don't just name the Link "Website". Name it "Best Coffee in Seattle" if you are a coffee shop optimization for that keyword. Context matters to search bots.

Crawl Budget

Since Vontage is lightweight HTML, Google bots crawl it easily. It's a fast way to get new content indexed by linking to it immediately from your high-traffic bio.
