The Digital Business Card: Why Paper Is Dead

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

88% of paper business cards are thrown away within a week. That is a lot of waste for a networking tool. Enter the Digital Business Card.

Always Up to Date

Changed jobs? Promoted? New phone number? With a Vontage link as your digital card, you update it once, and everyone who has your link sees the new info. Paper cards are obsolete the moment they are printed.

Rich Media

Paper can't play videos. Your digital card can link to your showreel, your calendar booking link (Calendly), and your social profiles. It gives a complete picture of who you are.

NFC Technology

Combine your Vontage link with an NFC tag or card (like Popl or Dot). You simply tap your phone against someone else's, and your bio link opens instantly on their screen. It's the "magic trick" of networking.
