Branding 101: Why You Need a Custom URL for Your Bio

Published on January 28, 2026 • 5 min read

Which looks more trustworthy?
A) `linktr.ee/user1234`
B) `vontage.app/?data=...`
C) `links.yourname.com`

The Psychology of Ownership

Using a custom platform or a clean URL signals that you own your platform. While Vontage uses URL parameters to keep costs at $0 (which results in longer URLs), you can easily mask this using a free domain forwarder or a bit.ly link to make it look branded.

Memorable = Shareable

If you are on a podcast and say "Visit my link in bio", nobody can find it. If you say "Visit jason.com/links", they can remember it. Branding is about owning mental real estate.

Consistency is Key

Your handle is `@jason`. Your website is `jason.com`. Your bio link should be `jason.bio`. Consistency builds trust, and trust drives conversion.
