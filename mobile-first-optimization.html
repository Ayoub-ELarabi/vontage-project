Mobile-First Optimization: Ensuring High Conversion on Small Screens

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

Social media traffic is almost exclusively mobile. If your bio link page looks good on desktop but breaks on iPhone Mini, you are losing money.

The "Thumb Zone"

Users hold phones with one hand. The most clickable area is the bottom half of the screen. Put your most profitable links within easy reach of the thumb.

Button Size Matters

Touch targets should be at least 44px tall. Vontage's buttons are designed to be "fat finger proof", ensuring users don't accidentally click the wrong link.

Readable Fonts

Small decorative scripts are impossible to read on a phone. Stick to Sans-Serif fonts like Inter, Roboto, or Outfit (which we use) at a minimum size of 16px.

Vertical Stacking

Never place buttons side-by-side unless they are icons. Vertical lists are the native scrolling behavior of mobile users. Embrace the feed.
