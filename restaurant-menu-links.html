The Touchless Menu: Bio Links for Restaurants and Cafes

Published on January 28, 2026 • 4 min read

Paying $50/mo for a digital menu hosting service? Stop.

The Free Alternative

Upload your menu PDF to Google Drive. Set permissions to "Anyone with link". Paste that link into your Vontage profile. Done. You now have a free, updateable digital menu.

Social Proof (Reviews)

Add a link "Rate us on Google Maps". Catch happy customers before they leave the table. A direct link increases review volume significantly.

Reservations

Link directly to OpenTable or Resy. Don't make hungry people search for the booking button.
