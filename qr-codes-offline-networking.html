QR Codes Are Back: Connecting Offline Networking to Online Bios

Published on January 28, 2026 • 4 min read

Thanks to the pandemic (menus!) and better phone cameras, QR codes are no longer a gimmick. They are a bridge between physical and digital worlds.

Use Cases for Bio Link QRs

1. **Business Cards**: Instead of printing your phone number (which changes), print a QR code to your Vontage link. You can update your contact info digitally without re-printing cards.
2. **Event Badges**: At conferences, let people scan your badge to connect instantly on LinkedIn.
3. **Product Packaging**: Put a QR code on your product that leads to a "How to Use" video or a review page via your bio link.

Generating Your Code

Vontage urls are short and clean, which generates simpler, easier-to-scan QR codes. Complex URLs create dense, hard-to-read codes. Always test your code on both iOS and Android before printing 1,000 flyers.
