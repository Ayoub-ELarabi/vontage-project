How to Sell Digital Products Directly From Your Bio Link

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

The "Creator Economy" is basically the "Digital Product Economy". Selling bits and bytes has zero marginal cost. But where do you put the "Buy" button when Instagram only gives you one link?

The Direct Link Strategy

The most common mistake is linking to a home page. If you mention an E-book in your video, your bio link should have a button that says "Get the E-Book", and it should go **directly** to the checkout page (Gumroad, Shopify, Stripe Payment Link).

Visual Cues for Sales

On your Vontage page, highlight your product link. Use emojis (ebook = 📚, course = 🎓). If possible, put it at the very top. Data shows that the top link gets 50% of total clicks.

Price Anchoring

Consider listing a "Free Preview" link right below the "Buy Full Course" link. This captures users who aren't ready to pay yet but are interested enough to download a sample (and give you their email).

Trust Signals

Your bio link is a landing page. It needs to look secure. Vontage's clean, ad-free aesthetic helps build that trust compared to spammy, ad-filled link shorteners.
