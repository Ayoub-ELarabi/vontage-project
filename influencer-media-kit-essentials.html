The Influencer Media Kit: Why Your Bio Link Is Key

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

When a brand manager visits your profile, they are looking for one thing: "Is this person professional?". A PDF media kit stuck in a Dropbox folder is too much friction. Your bio link is your new media kit.

What to Include

1. **Work With Me**: This should link to a contact form or booking calendar.
2. **Past Partnerships**: Link to your best branded content (TikToks/Reels) so they can see your production quality.
3. **Audience Demographics**: If possible, include a link to a simple graphic showing your reach and engagement.

Speed Wins Deals

If a brand has to DM you to get your rates, you've already lost. Use your bio link to streamline the negotiation process. "Click here for Rates" allows you to gate your pricing or show it openly to filter serious inquiries.

Professionalism Signals

Using a branded Vontage link instead of a raw Google Form link shows you take your business seriously. It says "I am a creator who understands marketing".
