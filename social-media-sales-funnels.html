Social Media Sales Funnels: The Beginner's Guide

Published on January 28, 2026 • 7 min read

A funnel is simply the path a customer takes to buy from you. On social media, it looks like this: Awareness (Content) -> Interest (Bio Link) -> Decision (Landing Page) -> Action (Purchase).

The Role of the Bio Link

Your Bio Link is the "Middle of Funnel" (MOFU). It filters people. Those who click are "interested". Your job is to make it easy for them to become "decided".

Segmenting Audiences

You probably have different types of followers. Beginners, Advanced, and Brands. Use your Vontage links to segment them.
- "Start Here (Free Guide)" -> For Beginners
- "Advanced Masterclass" -> For Pros
- "Work With Me" -> For Brands

Retargeting

If you use a custom domain with tracking pixels (advanced strategy), you can retarget anyone who visited your bio link with Facebook Ads. This is powerful because you know they are already warm leads.
