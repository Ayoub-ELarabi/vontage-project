Artists & Designers: Why a Bio Link Beats a Portfolio Site

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

Art Directors are busy. They don't have time to navigate your complex, animated React website just to see your drawings.

Curated Access

Use your bio link to route them to specific platforms. "Behance (Full Case Studies)" vs "Dribbble (WIPs)" vs "Instagram (Daily)". Let them choose the format they prefer.

Commission Status

Add a status indicator in your link. "Commissions: OPEN ✅" or "Booked until March ❌". This saves everyone time and managing expectations is key to professionalism.

Shop Drops

When you release a new print or zine, put that link at the top. Vontage's ability to update instantly is superior to waiting for a WordPress cache to clear.
