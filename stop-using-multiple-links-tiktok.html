Why You Should Stop Using Multiple Links in TikTok

Published on January 28, 2026 • 4 min read

Social media platforms like TikTok are built on speed. The average user spends less than 3 seconds deciding whether to engage with your profile. In this high-velocity environment, clutter is the enemy of conversion.

The Paradox of Choice

Psychologist Barry Schwartz coined the term "Paradox of Choice". When faced with too many options, humans tend to freeze and choose nothing. If your bio description is littered with three different URL strings (which aren't even clickable in the caption!), you are asking the user to do work. Users hate work.

One Link to Rule Them All

TikTok allows only **one** clickable link in your bio. That is it. If you have a YouTube channel, a merch store, and a Patreon, how do you choose? You don't. You use a Bio Link tool like Vontage.

Benefits of a Consolidated Link

  • **Clean User Experience:** A single URL looks trustworthy. Multi-line URL strings look like spam.
  • **Analytics Insight:** When everyone goes through one door, you can count them. Vontage (and similar tools) allow you to see exactly which destination is most popular.
  • **Cross-Promotion:** A user coming for your Merch might see your "New YouTube Video" link and click that too. You get two engagements for the price of one.

How to set it up

It takes literally seconds. Go to Vontage Home, add your links, and copy the generated URL. Paste that single URL into your TikTok website field. Done.
