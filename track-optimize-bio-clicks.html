Analytics 101: How to Track and Optimize Your Bio Clicks

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

Data is the difference between guessing and growing. Since Vontage is privacy-focused and serverless, we don't spy on your users. Here is how you can track them yourself.

The Power of UTM Parameters

Don't just link to `myshop.com`. Link to `myshop.com?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=biolink`. This tells Google Analytics exactly where the traffic came from. You can see not just clicks, but how many of those clicks turned into sales.

Shorteners with Stats

Tools like Bit.ly or Dub.co provide free click tracking. Wrap your destination URL in a shortener, then paste that shortened URL into Vontage. Now you have a dashboard of click counts.

Platform Insights

Instagram and TikTok provide "Website Taps" metrics in their professional dashboards. Compare this number to your total profile views to calculate your "Profile Conversion Rate". If it's below 1%, you need a better CTA in your bio.

A/B Testing

Try changing your button text from "Shop" to "Get 50% Off" for one week. Check your click counts. Keep the winner. Rinse and repeat.
