For Creators: Building a UGC Portfolio That Gets You Hired

Published on January 28, 2026 • 6 min read

UGC (User Generated Content) is a billion-dollar industry. Brands pay creators just to make videos, not to post them. But you need to prove you can do it.

Video-First Portfolio

A PDF portfolio is useless for video. Use your Vontage link to link directly to unlisted YouTube videos or a Google Drive folder of your best work. Label them clearly: "Skincare Example", "Tech Review Example".

Clear Contact Info

Put your business email at the very top. UGC is a B2B service. Treat it like one. Don't make brands DM you.

Show Results

If a previous video got 1M views, write that in the link title. "Viral Video Example (1.2M Views)". Brands buy results, not just pretty shots.
