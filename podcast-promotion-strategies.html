Podcast Promotion: Converting Listeners via Show Note Links

Published on January 28, 2026 • 4 min read

Discovery is the hardest part of podcasting. You need to capture listeners wherever they find you—Twitter, Instagram, or LinkedIn—and route them to Apple/Spotify instantly.

Smart Links

Instead of linking just to Apple Podcasts (ignoring Android users), use Vontage to create a "Listen Now" folder. Inside, list: Apple, Spotify, Google, and YouTube. Let the user choose their player.

Show Notes Redux

Instagram captions are not searchable. Put detailed show notes or "Key Takeaways" as a blog post or PDF linked in your bio. "Get the cheat sheet for this episode" is a powerful hook.

Guest Features

When you have a guest, create a specific link for their book or website. It adds value to the guest (making them likely to share) and your audience.
